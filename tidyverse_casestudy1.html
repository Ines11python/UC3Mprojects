  1. How to convert it to tidy data? Do whatever you consider to end up with a three column table: religion, income and people
tidy_relig_income <- 
  relig_income |> 
  pivot_longer(cols = "<$10k":"Don't know/refused", 
               names_to = "income", 
               values_to = "people" )
